## Background

During my first year of work as a software developer, I was told to describe things in an "overall" aspect and think each problem in a "system level" at the very beginning. However, at that time, I did not realize it is correlated with system design. 

To conclude, it will be better to practice sys design in the following way:
The design session usually last for about **45 minutes**. During the session, the interviewee is expected to steering the conversation.

Some questions are expected to be paid attention to:
What are the constrains?
What kind of input does the system want?
